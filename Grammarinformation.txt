
Grammar

The grammar of this language for this moment accepts 2 types of statements: 
1. arithmatic expressions. 
2. string statments ( written between quotes) 

All implemented expressions return  a result or the given string. 

You can have as many expressions as you want but they have to be separated by a semicolon (;) .
White space and new lines are ignored. 
All the results are printed at the end of the programm. 

Here is the list with already implemented arithmetic operations:  
1. Addition + 
2. Substraction -
3. Multiplication * 
4. Division /
5. Modulo   mod
